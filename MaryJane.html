The one and only in-law

Posted by MaryJane on April 1, 2024

David is kind and generous to all. He is no respecter of persons and welcomes all into his home. He is willing to volunteer, lead, and do the hard stuff.

He is a great example of diligence and willingness to work. He has taught his children well the value of work and the importance of grit. I see these fruits firsthand being married to Joseph.

He has a way of making you feel comfortable doing things out of your comfort zone (what an oxymoron is that!). Since knowing him I have snowmobiled in deep Idahoan snows, repeled steep cliffs in Southern Utah, rock climbed canyon walls, and tried my hand (or rather legs) at skiing. All of which I enjoyed!

Overall, he is a good, faithful, man that desires the best for his family. How blessed we are to call him father!
